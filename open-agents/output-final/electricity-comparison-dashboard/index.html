PA Solar Incentives Available

  • Federal Solar ITC: 30% tax credit on full system cost
  • PA Sunshine Program: Up to $5,000 rebate for systems up to 10 kW
  • Net Metering: Earn credits for excess power sent to the grid
  • SRECs: Earn Solar Renewable Energy Credits for every 1,000 kWh generated (~$30-50/SREC in PA)
  • PA Solar for All: $156M federal grant program for qualifying households

This matrix is what the user's "is polyvagal even the right framework?" question reduces to: for each phenomenon, count how many frameworks can predict it and which of those frameworks has the strongest empirical grounding. The answer is consistent: PVT has no unique explanatory advantage on the phenomena that matter.

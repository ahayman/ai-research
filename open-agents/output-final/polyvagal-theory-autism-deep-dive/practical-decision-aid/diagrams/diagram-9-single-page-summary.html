The Single-Page Summary — Four Panels

The most compressed possible statement of where this deep dive lands.

1

The theory is contested

  • Grossman 2023 in Biological Psychology: five core premises of PVT inconsistent with comparative neuroanatomy.
  • 2026 "Untenable" consensus: 39 international experts (Grossman + 38 invited specialists) co-author rejection.
  • Specific challenged claims: three-tier vagal hierarchy; mammalian-only myelinated vagus; dorsal-vagal as collapse substrate; neuroception as unique mechanism.
  • Porges has responded; the debate is active.
2

The data is real

  • Cheng 2020 meta-analysis of 34 studies: autistic adults show lower baseline parasympathetic HRV (g ≈ −0.52) and lower RSA reactivity to social stress (g ≈ −0.72).
  • These are robust, replicated, real measurements.
  • The findings exist independently of which theoretical framework explains them.
  • Kushki 2022 shows heterogeneity — subgroups matter; the group mean masks variation.
3

Alternative frameworks explain the data

  • Neurovisceral Integration (Thayer & Lane) — predicts the same HRV findings via vmPFC → amygdala → vagus; mainstream in autonomic neuroscience.
  • Predictive processing / active inference (Friston, Seth, Barrett) — dominant contemporary framework for interoception and emotion.
  • Allostatic load (Sterling, McEwen) — the framework Raymaker 2020 / AASPIRE actually uses for autistic burnout.
  • Constructed emotion (Barrett) — explicitly contradicts PVT's hardwired-state architecture.
4

Some practices work for non-PVT reasons

  • Slow paced breathing — cardiovascular physiology, decades before PVT.
  • Humming — Lundberg nasal NO research; laryngeal vagal afferents.
  • Cold face splash — trigeminal-vagal diving reflex, well-characterized.
  • Co-regulation — attachment theory (Bowlby, Ainsworth, Sroufe), 1960s onward.
  • Sensory accommodation & demand reduction — allostatic load framework, autism-specific.
  • None of these require polyvagal theory to be correct.

The resolution

Polyvagal theory as proposed by Porges is contested at the level of its evolutionary and anatomical claims. The data that motivated the theory is real. Less-contested frameworks explain the same data. Some practices marketed under polyvagal language work for non-PVT reasons. The wellness industry has overreached significantly. For autistic burnout specifically, allostatic load is the empirically anchored framework.

Action plan: Tier A tonight. Tier B with pre-specified endpoints. Skip Tier C. Avoid Tier D. Especially careful with Tier E. Hold the framework lightly.

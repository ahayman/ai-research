The pattern
gammaCore is the only consumer-accessible non-invasive VNS device with substantive FDA clearance — and that clearance is limited to specific neurological indications, not autism or anxiety. The rest of the consumer market is sold as "general wellness," a regulatory category that does not require FDA clearance at all. None of the wellness-category devices has a sham-controlled RCT in autism. None has demonstrated equivalence to gammaCore's published evidence base. The "polyvagal" or "vagus nerve" label on a product box is a marketing choice, not a regulatory or clinical claim.

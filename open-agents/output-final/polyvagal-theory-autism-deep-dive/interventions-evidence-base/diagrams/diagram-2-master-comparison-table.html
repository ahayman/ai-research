Reading this table. Tier classifications combine evidence strength with mechanism plausibility. Two interventions can both be Tier C for different reasons — one because trials are still preliminary (taVNS), another because the mechanism is speculative (Apollo Neuro). The "Best Use" column is calibrated for the user (autistic adult with burnout). Cost ranges are 2026 USD, approximate.

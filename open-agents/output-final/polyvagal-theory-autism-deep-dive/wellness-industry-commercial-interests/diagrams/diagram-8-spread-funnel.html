How the Theory Spreads — 120,000+ Google Results, Five Stages

From specialty journal to consumer Instagram reel. Each stage amplifies reach, loses hedging, gains certainty.

The compounding pattern. Each stage borrows credibility from the stage above it. The trade book cites the academic paper (Stage 2 inherits credibility from Stage 1). The training cites the trade book (Stage 3 inherits from Stage 2). The social media post simplifies the training (Stage 4 inherits from Stage 3). The consumer device cites all of the above (Stage 5 inherits from everywhere). By Stage 5, the original Stage 1 hedging is invisible, but the original Stage 1 academic citation is still being invoked as the warrant for the claim. The 2023–2026 academic controversy — which casts substantive doubt on the Stage 1 foundation — has not propagated down through the stages. A polyvagal-branded product sold in May 2026 may still implicitly rest on premises that 38 international experts argued are falsified earlier the same year.
Five stages of theory migration. Reach increases at each stage. Hedging decreases at each stage. The "120,000+ Google results" figure (Atlanta Wellness Collective) reflects the cumulative output of Stages 2–5. The Stage 1 academic foundation is being challenged in 2023–2026 but the downstream stages largely operate as if the foundation is settled.

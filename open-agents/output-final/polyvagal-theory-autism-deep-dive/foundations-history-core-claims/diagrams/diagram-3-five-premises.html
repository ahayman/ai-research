The Five Core Premises of Polyvagal Theory

The load-bearing claims that together constitute the theory — the same five that Grossman 2023 and the 2026 "Untenable" consensus target, and that the 2025 Porges update defends.

Attribution note. Porges himself does not typically present the theory as a numbered list of five premises — his writing is narrative-philosophical. The "five premises" framing was crystallized in Grossman's 2023 critique paper and has since become the standard structuring device. The 2025 Porges update implicitly defends roughly this enumeration; the 2026 "Untenable" consensus targets it directly.
Premise 1

Hierarchical autonomic organization

The autonomic nervous system is organized as a three-tier hierarchy — ventral vagal, sympathetic, dorsal vagal — that operates in a phylogenetic order, with newer tiers inhibiting older tiers under conditions of safety.

What this replaces The classical two-branch (sympathetic / parasympathetic) view inherited from Cannon and Selye.
Premise 2

Mammalian-specific myelinated ventral vagus

Mammals uniquely possess a myelinated, fast-conducting cardiac vagal pathway originating in the nucleus ambiguus — an evolutionary innovation absent from reptiles, fish, and other non-mammalian vertebrates per PVT.

Key evolutionary claim Migration of cardioinhibitory neurons from dorsal motor nucleus to nucleus ambiguus is uniquely mammalian.
Premise 3

Neuroception

The nervous system continuously and sub-consciously evaluates environmental and visceral cues for safety, danger, or life-threat — a process distinct from conscious perception and capable of triggering autonomic state shifts without awareness.

Term introduced Porges 2004, Zero to Three.
Premise 4

Integrated social engagement system

Cranial nerves V, VII, IX, X, and XI form a coordinated brainstem network — integrating heart, face, voice, middle ear, and head orientation — that supports mammalian social interaction. The myelinated ventral vagus is the cardiac component of this system.

Clinical extension Listening Project Protocol → Safe and Sound Protocol (SSP) acoustic interventions.
Premise 5

Physiological state as intervening variable

Autonomic state — measured via biomarkers like RSA, vagal efficiency, and weighted coherence — mediates between environmental cues and behavioral output. State is not just a correlate of behavior; it constrains what behavior is available.

Falsifiability claim Porges argues this generates testable predictions about state, biomarker, and behavior.

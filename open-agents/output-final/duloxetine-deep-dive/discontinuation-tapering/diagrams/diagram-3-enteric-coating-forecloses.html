Why the Enteric Coating Forecloses the Obvious Workarounds

Duloxetine is harder to taper than drugs with an identical half-life, and the reason is chemical rather than commercial. One property at the start of the chain removes every kitchen-table solution at the end of it.

The property
Acid-labile
Duloxetine degrades below about pH 5.5. Stomach acid destroys it.
→
The consequence
Enteric-coated pellets
Each bead is individually coated. The coating is the only thing carrying the drug intact past the stomach.
→
The result
Break the coating, lose the drug
Anything that damages the coating hands the drug straight to the acid it was designed to survive.

What this rules out

✗Crush or chew it

The intuitive way to make a smaller dose.

Why not: crushing destroys the coating. Stomach acid then degrades the drug, so the dose you get is not a smaller dose, it is an unpredictable one.
✗Dissolve it in water

How you would taper most liquids-capable drugs at home.

Why not: same failure. Dispersing the beads in water strips the coating. You cannot make a duloxetine liquid at your kitchen table.
✗Cut it in half

What you would do with a scored tablet.

Why not: it is not a tablet. It is a capsule full of coated beads, so there is nothing to score and nothing to halve.
✗Take it every other day

What a busy prescriber reaches for when there is no smaller capsule.

Why not: specifically contraindicated at a ~12h half-life. This is the worst option on the page and it has its own diagram.

What survives

✓Open the capsule, count the beads

Each bead keeps its own coating, so the coating survives if you do not crush anything.

The catch: ~250 beads per 30mg capsule, roughly 8 per milligram. Bead counts and bead sizes both vary between capsules, so accuracy degrades exactly at the low doses where precision matters most. No trials exist.
✓A compounded liquid or custom capsules

A pharmacy protects the drug by another route, so you get real precision below 10mg.

The catch: needs a prescriber willing to write it and a compounding pharmacy willing to make it. This is the thing worth asking about now, while it is hypothetical.
And the floor: the smallest capsule made is 20mg
US strengths are 20 / 30 / 60mg delayed-release. Applesauce and apple juice are acceptable vehicles; chocolate pudding is not.
The chain matters more than any single link. A drug that were merely short-half-life, without the coating, could be tapered with a cheap compounded suspension or a pill cutter. Duloxetine's acid-lability forces the coating, the coating forbids crushing and liquefying, and a 20mg minimum capsule closes the last door. That leaves counting coated beads or paying a pharmacy to solve the problem properly. Both are workable. Neither is free, and neither has ever been tested in a trial.

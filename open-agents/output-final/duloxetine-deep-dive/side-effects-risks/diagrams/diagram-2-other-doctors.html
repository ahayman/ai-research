The Ones That Come From Other Doctors

Three drugs are absolute contraindications with duloxetine. The reason they sit at the top of the safety page is not their pharmacology. It is their delivery route.

You may be the only person in the room who knows you are on duloxetine.

And in one of these three situations, you will not be able to say so.

Methylene blue

Potent reversible MAO-A inhibitor · FDA safety communication, 2011
Who gives itAn anaesthetist, intraoperatively — often for parathyroid or urological localisation.
Why it catches peopleIt is not a psychiatric drug and is not thought of as one. The surgical team is solving a visualisation problem, not reviewing your antidepressant list.
You are unconscious

Linezolid

Antibiotic · reversible MAOI
Who gives itAn infectious-disease team, treating a resistant Gram-positive infection.
Why it catches peopleIt is reached for when other antibiotics have failed, often urgently, by a team focused on the organism rather than on your regular medications.
You may be acutely unwell

MAOIs

phenelzine · tranylcypromine · isocarboxazid · selegiline
Who gives itA psychiatrist, usually for treatment-resistant depression — or a neurologist, since selegiline is used in Parkinson's.
Why it catches peopleThe washout is long and asymmetric: 5 days off duloxetine before starting an MAOI, but 14 days off an MAOI before starting duloxetine.
Usually planned — lowest risk of the three

Where the information chain breaks

Your GP
prescribed duloxetine
→
Your record
correct, and not in the room
→
A different clinician
different service, urgent context
→
The drug is given

Every link is doing its job. The failure is structural, not anybody's mistake — which is exactly why the fix has to be structural too.

What this actually implies

This is the single item on the whole list most worth physically carrying rather than remembering. Not because the pharmacology is worse than tramadol's, but because it is the only one where your knowledge cannot reach the moment of decision.

  • In a form that survives you being unconscious or acutely unwell: a card in a wallet, a phone lock screen, a bracelet, a note you can hand over instead of recite.
  • If methylene blue is genuinely unavoidable in an emergency, the guidance is: stop duloxetine immediately, resume no sooner than 24 hours after the last dose.

Substances to Avoid

‚ö†Ô∏è Kratom (Mitragyna speciosa) - NOT RECOMMENDED

67% of 2,798 surveyed users report using kratom for anxiety. However, significant safety concerns exist:

  • Addiction potential: Signs of compulsive use and withdrawal symptoms reported
  • Mental health risks: May exacerbate disorders including psychosis
  • Contamination: FDA warnings about salmonella, heavy metals, and fentanyl-laced products
  • Unregulated: Product quality highly variable; not FDA-approved
  • Rebound effects: Emotional crashes, irritability, heightened anxiety after use

Recommendation: Seek safer, evidence-backed alternatives listed above.

Medical Disclaimer: This data dashboard is for informational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice. Most substances require further large-scale clinical trials to establish optimal dosing and long-term safety. The supplement industry remains poorly regulated. Always consult a healthcare provider before beginning any supplementation regimen, especially if you are pregnant, nursing, taking medications, or have underlying health conditions.

Evidence-Quality Grid — Each Link in the Bidirectional Loop

The bidirectional loop is built from many distinct empirical claims, each with its own evidence level. The honest reading: some links are strong, some are moderate, some are cross-sectional with replication, some are theoretical extrapolations. Trust the unified loop only as much as the constituent links can carry it.

Maintenance & chronicity
Alexithymia is a maintaining factor in chronic PTSD Strong Frewen 2008 meta-analysis (12 studies, n=1,095) — large effect; Honkalampi multi-wave longitudinal; refugee long-term studies; Cloitre lineage
Alexithymic PTSD has separable neural signature (cognitive-affective phenotype) Moderate Frewen systematic review (S0165032720327968); default-mode/mPFC alterations alongside salience-network disturbances

CPTSD status & first-responder case

Iatrogenic risk
Body-focused trauma therapy carries amplified iatrogenic risk in alexithymia Theoretical-extrapolation Britton adverse-effects work (PMC8845498); parsing-deficit subgroup is real; Treleaven framework is trauma-focused not alexithymia-focused — alexithymia-specific empirical test thin
Synthesis
Bidirectional feedback loop model (unified) Theoretical-integrative Convergence across Schimmenti, Caretti, Cloitre, Lanius, Frewen — but not directly tested as a single longitudinal mechanism. The pieces are empirical; the integration is synthesis.
How to read this grid. The bidirectional loop is best supported at the maintenance and treatment-moderation links (strong evidence) and at the developmental-origins limb (covered in Cat 5, well-established). It is most thinly supported at the unified longitudinal mechanism level — the convergence is theoretical synthesis, not single-study confirmation. The pre-trauma vulnerability link sits at moderate evidence with one well-designed small prospective (McCaslin) and converging mixed-quality work. Re-traumatization is well-replicated cross-sectionally but causally underdetermined.

Honest stance for the user: believe the loop, but believe each link with weight proportional to its specific evidence base.

How to read this grid Read across each row. The endpoints (links 1–3 and 8) have strong empirical anchors. The middle of the cascade (links 4–7) is where empirical confidence drops and clinical/theoretical scaffolding does the work. Specifically: link 6 (chronic invalidation around the event) and link 7 (repeated meltdowns as themselves traumagenic) have NO direct empirical test. The cascade as a unified causal chain (link 8 viewed as mechanism rather than as endpoint) has not been validated by a single longitudinal study. Empirical at the link level; theoretical at the unification level.

Critical reading note. A reader who uses this matrix to make a therapy decision is making a theoretically-informed choice, NOT a choice grounded in subtype-stratified RCT evidence. Most cells are theoretical extrapolation. The empirically-tested cells are flagged. Held loosely, the matrix is useful as a navigation toolkit; reified into a clinical recommendation, it overstates what the evidence base supports.

The Therapy Landscape for Alexithymia

Twelve approaches with empirically-grounded evidence-base sizes. Largest evidence base does not mean best fit for any given subtype — that is a separate question (see Diagram 3). Cameron 2024 systematic review: 21 RCT arms, 1,251 participants, ~67% CBT.

CBT
Cognitive Behavioural Therapy
Granularity-building, affect-labelling, structured emotion vocabularies, journaling. Most-tested approach.
Large evidence base
DBT
Dialectical Behaviour Therapy
Emotion regulation, mindfulness, distress tolerance, interpersonal effectiveness modules. BPD-comorbid populations especially.
Salles 2022 review
ACT
Acceptance & Commitment Therapy
Defusion + values-based action; doesn't require knowing what you feel. Theoretical fit for parsing-deficit.
Medium evidence base
MBT
Mentalization-Based Therapy
Mentalizing scaffold for "I don't know exactly what this is." Strong fit for parsing-deficit.
Fischer-Kern 2018
EAET
Lumley's Emotional Awareness & Expression Therapy
Scaffolded explicit emotion-naming for chronic pain + alexithymia. 2024 RCT vs CBT in older vets.
Multiple chronic-pain RCTs
EFT
Emotion-Focused Therapy (Greenberg)
Chair work, two-chair dialogues, in-session deepening. Strong theoretical fit but mixed empirical TAS-20 effects.
Mixed empirical fit
Somatic / SE / MABT
Body-based modalities
Levine SE, Pat Ogden Sensorimotor, Cynthia Price MABT (scaffolded for interoceptively-difficult clients).
Small evidence base
Schema / CFT
Schema therapy & Compassion-Focused
Targets early maladaptive schemas (Schema), shame/self-criticism (CFT). Conceptual fit for developmental-origin cases.
Small alexithymia-specific evidence
Psychodynamic
Long-term & ISTDP short-term
Transference-based; original psychoanalytic frame for alexithymia. Some short-term protocols show TAS-20 reductions.
Mixed alexithymia-specific evidence
Levant ART
Alexithymia Reduction Treatment
Manualized psychoeducational group format for normative male alexithymia. Real, manualized, small evidence base.
Small evidence base
Pharmacological
Intranasal oxytocin (preliminary)
High-alexithymia subgroups benefit more on social-cognitive tasks. Luminet 2011; Quirin & Carter. Not approved for alexithymia.
Preliminary
IFS
Internal Family Systems
One therapy among many. See companion deep dive for in-depth treatment. Adapts via externalization, granularity-building.
See IFS deep dive
Evidence-base size badges: Large = systematically reviewed with multiple RCTs; Medium = multiple trials, partial systematic-review coverage; Small = case-series + few trials; Preliminary = signal exists but lacks established evidence; Cross-link = covered in companion deep dive.

Important caveat: Evidence-base size measures empirical coverage, not "best therapy." Most trials are subtype-blind. Subtype-matched indications (Diagram 3) are theoretical extrapolations, not findings from head-to-head subtype-stratified RCTs.

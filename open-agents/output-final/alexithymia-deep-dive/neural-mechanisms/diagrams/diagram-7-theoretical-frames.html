How to read the frames. Six theoretical frames sit at different abstraction layers and make different claims about “where alexithymia lives”. They are not a horserace — they describe the same phenotype at different levels, and they imply different therapy classes. The Hobson 2019 multiroute model is the most useful integrative frame: it acknowledges that all the upstream loci are real for someone, and that different alexithymics sit at different routes. The user’s task is not to pick the “right” frame but to identify which route(s) their own alexithymia most plausibly lives on, then choose intervention class accordingly.

# Diagram 3: 8 Cs of Self — Radial Layout (with 5 Ps as outer ring)

**Type**: Mermaid mindmap
**Purpose**: Show the eight canonical qualities of Self as facets of one center, with the supplementary 5 Ps as an outer ring. Visualizes that Self is one thing with many qualities, not a list.

```mermaid
mindmap
  root((SELF))
    Curiosity
      ::icon(fa fa-search)
    Calm
      ::icon(fa fa-leaf)
    Compassion
      ::icon(fa fa-heart)
    Clarity
      ::icon(fa fa-eye)
    Courage
      ::icon(fa fa-mountain)
    Confidence
      ::icon(fa fa-anchor)
    Creativity
      ::icon(fa fa-paint-brush)
    Connectedness
      ::icon(fa fa-link)
    "5 Ps (supplementary)"
      Presence
      Patience
      Persistence
      Perspective
      Playfulness
```

**Caption**: The 8 Cs of Self in IFS — Curiosity, Calm, Compassion, Clarity, Courage, Confidence, Creativity, Connectedness — with the supplementary 5 Ps (Presence, Patience, Persistence, Perspective, Playfulness) as an outer set. Schwartz arrived at the 8 Cs by noticing that when his clients' parts agreed to step back, the felt qualities of what remained kept clustering around words starting with C. The list is canonical (published by the IFS Institute as a one-page reference) and stable across primary and secondary IFS literature. The 5 Ps are treated as supplementary rather than core — they appear in third-party clinician summaries but not in the IFS Institute's own 8 Cs reference document. Both lists are descriptive of one thing (Self), not catalogues of separable virtues.

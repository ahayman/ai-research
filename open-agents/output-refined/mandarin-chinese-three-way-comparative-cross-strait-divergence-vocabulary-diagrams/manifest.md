## Diagram Inventory

### 1. Cross-Strait Vocabulary Divergence Showcase

Each with Putonghua vs. Guoyu examples showing characters, pinyin, and IPA. Attitude perception section showing how each variety sounds to speakers of the other.

- **Features**: Dark/light mode, four feature categories, attitude perception cards

### 4. Japanese Loanword Layer

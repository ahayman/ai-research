The pattern: One device is FDA-cleared (gammaCore, by prescription, for specific headache indications). The other five operate under the FDA general wellness device framework, which requires no clinical efficacy demonstration. The wellness-branded consumer products use the language of "vagus nerve stimulation" interchangeably with FDA-cleared medical devices, but the regulatory status, evidence base, and indication scope are categorically different. Apollo Neuro has more independent-looking data than peers; Hapbee has the least mechanistically plausible claim. None of the consumer wellness devices on this list is supported by an independent sham-controlled RCT for the autism-spectrum populations the user is concerned with.

## Visualization Descriptions

### 1. Anxiety Prevalence Comparison (viz-1)
Bar chart comparing anxiety prevalence rates between autistic individuals and the general population, broken down by children and adults.

### 2. Evidence Levels by Intervention (viz-2)
Horizontal bar chart showing the strength of scientific evidence for each intervention type, color-coded by evidence level.

### 3. Sensory Intervention Outcomes (viz-3)
Grouped bar chart comparing outcomes between the Sensory Regulation Classroom Package (SRCP) intervention and control groups.

### 4. PEERS Program Outcomes (viz-4)
Line chart showing long-term retention of social skills gains from baseline through 3-5 year follow-up.

### 5. Environmental Modifications (viz-5)
Interactive card-based infographic displaying the six key components of autism-friendly environmental design.

### 6. Treatment Duration Timeline (viz-6)
Visual timeline showing recommended treatment duration for different interventions, with color-coded bars.

The composite picture. No program scores "good" on any criterion. The most-cited program (Gupta AIR 2023) reaches "partial" on two criteria. DNRS and Primal Trust have essentially no published evidence base. Lightning Process has the worst trial-conduct record (SMILE outcome-switching, journal correction). Across 10 criteria × 4 programs = 40 cells, exactly zero are "good." This is not how clinically robust interventions look at the evidence-quality level. It is, however, how commercial wellness products typically present when evaluated rigorously.

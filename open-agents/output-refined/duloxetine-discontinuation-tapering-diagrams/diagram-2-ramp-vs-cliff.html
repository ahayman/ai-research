This is why the argument fails. "Don't go up, because coming down will be harder" treats the two panels as one journey. They are not. Going to 30 or 60mg adds the left panel, which is gentle and fully provisioned, in front of the right panel, which does not change at all. The cliff does not get taller. It is already there, and at 20mg you are already standing on its edge.

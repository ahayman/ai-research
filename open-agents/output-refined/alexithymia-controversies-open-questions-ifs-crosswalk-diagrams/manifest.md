## Universal corrections applied throughout

- PMC6089261 = Nicholson, Williams, Grainger, Christensen, Calvo-Merino & Gaigg (2018), independent group challenge
- PMC7076086 = Aaron, Blain, Snodgress & Park (2020), quadratic relationship
- Bird/Murphy lineage continues multidomain interoception failure thesis; only autism-specific claim was walked back
- Hobson 2019 multiroute as most current synthesis
- Britton 2021 (58/37/83%) ≠ Goldberg 2022 (32.3/50/10.4/1.2%)
- ADIE: small effect d=0.30, MCID missed, 50% attrition
- "English et al. 2020" = Schouten, Boiger, Kirchner-Häusler, Uchida & Mesquita 2020
- DBT review = Salles et al. 2022 (not Carmona)
- Shapiro 2011 framed as critical of "professional alexithymia," not endorsing
- Grynberg 2010 hedged; Pisani 2020 the cleaner lead for "preserved cognitive empathy"

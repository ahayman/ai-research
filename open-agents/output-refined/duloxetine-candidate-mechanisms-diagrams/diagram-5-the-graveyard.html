STAR EXHIBIT
DRN 5-HT1A sensitization model		 INVERTED. 5-HT1A autoreceptor desensitisation IS the sensitization in the model (Rozeske 2011), and chronic SSRIs produce the same receptor state at 14 days (Zhang 2025). The proposed cure is the proposed disease. Read as proposed, it predicts duloxetine INSTALLS the helpless state. His Saturday is a counterexample to it, not a confirmation. Duration also fails 3–5× (24–72h vs his 7–14 days) LOGIC
"Phasic:tonic ratio up = acute reactivity up" MISREADING OF THE SOURCE. The excitatory component was left INTACT; the ratio rose because tonic fell and the inhibitory component grew. And the only chronic-duloxetine LC recording says the excitatory response goes DOWN LOGIC
BNST sustained-threat duration story TIMESCALE OFF ~1000×. "Longer-lasting" means minutes to hours in a startle paradigm. His episodes last 1–2 weeks MEASUREMENT
Propranolol / PTSD prophylaxis parallel ANTI-PARALLEL. Propranolol blunts acute reactivity; his rose to >140 bpm. It predicts the opposite of his headline observation. It also needs a memory with propositional content, which his episodes lack LOGIC
The escitalopram occupancy comparison ("less serotonin") CROSS-STUDY ARTIFACT. Single-dose vs steady-state, thalamus vs midbrain, PET vs SPECT, two labs. Escitalopram 10mg's own between-study range (59–88%) is 3× the claimed effect and contains duloxetine's entirely MEASUREMENT
Non-fearful panic disorder as a diagnostic match DURATION: MINUTES VS WEEKS. Also a category error: NFPD is absent fear; he reports absent cognitions. Cardiology population. The CBT-mismatch argument survives; the diagnostic claim does not MEASUREMENT
"20mg is sub-therapeutic on both transporters" SINGLE-DOSE-VS-STEADY-STATE CONFUSION plus CROSS-REGION. He is at ~75% striatal at steady state MEASUREMENT
Atomoxetine "40% is the normal NET working range" WRONG TRACER. Traces to Logan 2007, which used [11C]MRB rather than [18F]FMeNER-D2, reports 24–31%, and whose own authors blame signal-to-noise. Contradicted by same-tracer data MEASUREMENT
The migraine-prophylaxis analogy COCHRANE SAYS NO. "The use of SSRIs and SNRIs for migraine prophylaxis is not supported by evidence." No duloxetine trial met inclusion criteria at all LOGIC
REM rebound explains the vivid dreams A DISCONTINUATION PHENOMENON. He is at steady state and not coming off. Pace-Schott 2001 dissociates recall from intensity. Nobody knows why antidepressants intensify dreams LOGIC
"NE rises therefore sleep worsens" OUTCOME DATA CONTRADICTS. In the only fixed-dose trial testing 20/60/120mg, insomnia went down 20→60. When mechanism and outcome data disagree, trust the outcome data MEASUREMENT
"His nap-failure is the Watson ME/CFS finding from the inside" WATSON'S TWINS FELL ASLEEP IN 8.9 MINUTES. That is not nap failure. Where the ME/CFS MSLT literature is directional it points the other way: Neu 2008 found CFS patients fall asleep significantly faster than controls MEASUREMENT
Why the star exhibit matters more than the rest. Same receptor, same nucleus, same end-state. One literature calls it learned helplessness. The other calls it the antidepressant mechanism. Nobody has reconciled them. That is not a fact about him. It is a fact about the field, and it is the clearest available demonstration that an elegant mechanistic story can be exactly backwards while sounding exactly right. [primary, verified ×3]
No fabricated citations exist anywhere in eight research documents. Every failure was a misreading of a real paper, or a real paper wrongly discarded. That is the more dangerous mode, because a resolving PMID looks like verification and is not. The rule the project ended up with: a resolving PMID is not verification. Check what the paper says.

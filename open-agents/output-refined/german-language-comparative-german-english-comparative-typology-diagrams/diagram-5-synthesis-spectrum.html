Analytic (Isolating)

One morpheme per word. Grammar via word order, particles, separate function words. Chinese is the canonical example.

Fusional

One affix encodes multiple categories simultaneously. German "dem" = dative + masculine + singular. Most Indo-European languages.

Agglutinative

Multiple affixes, each encoding one category, strung together transparently. Turkish: ev-ler-im-den = house-PL-my-from.

Polysynthetic

Entire sentences packed into single words with many morphemes. Inuktitut can express a full clause as one word.

Semantic Field Clustering of Radicals

Radicals organize into natural meaning domains, reflecting how Chinese culture categorizes the world

💧
Water & Liquid Domain
Includes rivers, weather, fluids, emotions that "flow"
氵water 雨rain 冫ice
Characters: 河 river, 海 sea, 清 clear, 雪 snow, 冷 cold, 泪 tears
🔥
Fire & Heat Domain
Cooking, burning, light, warmth
火fire 灬fire dots 日sun
Characters: 热 hot, 烧 burn, 煮 cook, 烈 fierce, 明 bright, 晴 sunny
🌿
Plant & Wood Domain
Trees, grasses, grains, bamboo — the largest radical family
艹grass 木wood 禾grain 竹bamboo 米rice
Characters: 花 flower, 林 forest, 秋 autumn, 笔 brush, 粉 powder, 药 medicine
🏔
Earth & Terrain Domain
Land, mountains, stones, fields
土earth 山mountain 石stone 田field 穴cave
Characters: 地 ground, 峰 peak, 碑 stele, 界 boundary, 空 empty/sky
🔧
Metal & Craft Domain
Metals, tools, blades, weaving
钅metal 刂knife 糸silk/thread 巾cloth 瓦tile
Characters: 铁 iron, 银 silver, 刻 carve, 纸 paper, 织 weave, 帽 hat
🦴
Body & Senses Domain
Body parts, physical actions, perception
扌hand 口mouth 目eye 耳ear 足foot 骨bone 月flesh
Characters: 打 hit, 吃 eat, 看 see, 听 hear, 跑 run, 脑 brain, 肝 liver
💭
Mind & Speech Domain
Thoughts, emotions, language, knowledge
忄heart/mind 讠speech 见see/view
Characters: 情 emotion, 快 fast/happy, 说 speak, 读 read, 认 recognize, 观 observe
👥
Human & Society Domain
People, women, shelter, clothing
亻person 女woman 宀roof 衤clothes 贝shell/money
Characters: 他 he, 妈 mom, 家 home, 被 quilt, 财 wealth, 货 goods
🐾
Animal & Life Domain
Mammals, birds, insects, fish
犭animal 虫insect 鸟bird 鱼fish 马horse
Characters: 猫 cat, 蛇 snake, 鸡 chicken, 鲜 fresh, 骑 ride
🚶
Movement & Action Domain
Walking, transport, force, opposition
辶walk/road 车vehicle 力power 弓bow
Characters: 道 path/way, 运 transport, 动 move, 张 stretch
🌙
Sky & Spirit Domain
Weather, time, religion, divination
日sun/day 月moon 礻spirit 雨rain
Characters: 时 time, 期 period, 神 god, 祝 wish, 雷 thunder
🏠
Shelter & Food Domain
Buildings, enclosures, cooking, eating
门gate 囗enclosure 饣food 酉wine
Characters: 问 ask, 国 country, 饭 rice/meal, 醉 drunk

Some radicals span multiple domains: 月 serves as both "moon" (sky domain) and "flesh/body part" (body domain) depending on character context. Radical 贝 (shell) = money because cowrie shells were ancient Chinese currency.

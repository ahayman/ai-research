## Diagram Descriptions

### Diagram 1: Timeline of the Polyvagal Critique (1994 → 2026)
- **Type**: Horizontal timeline with color-coded event nodes
- **Elements**: 13 milestone events; 7 event categories (theory establishment, early critique, empirical refutation, anatomical critique, expert consensus, Porges response, field discourse)
- **Key Insight**: The 2026 "Untenable" consensus is the consolidation of a 20-year arc of cumulative critique, not a sudden 2026 revelation
- **Accessibility**: Each node has explicit year, title, and descriptive paragraph; color coding supplemented by text labels

### Diagram 2: The Five Premises Under Attack
- **Type**: 5-row grid; each row = PVT claim | counter-evidence | status badge
- **Elements**: Premise 1 (RSA = vagal tone), Premise 2 (DMN/NA cardiac split), Premise 3 (uniquely mammalian myelinated vagus), Premise 4 (vagal-sociality coevolution), Premise 5 (historical literature support)
- **Status colors**: Red (falsified) — Premises 1, 3, 4; Amber (substantially weakened) — Premise 2; Amber (contested) — Premise 5
- **Key Insight**: The most directly testable empirical claims have been clearly falsified; the dual-architecture claim is substantially weakened; the historical-interpretation claim is contested

### Diagram 3: The Lungfish Problem
- **Type**: Vertebrate phylogeny with myelination-presence bars by clade
- **Elements**: 7 vertebrate clades (sharks → bony fish → lungfish → amphibians → reptiles → birds → mammals); each with myelination percentage where quantified
- **Key Insight**: Myelinated cardiac vagal fibers are not a mammalian innovation; the lungfish result (Monteiro 2018) is the cleanest single empirical refutation of PVT Premise 3
- **Highlighted rows**: Lungfish (refutation point) and Mammals (clade PVT claims uniqueness for)

### Diagram 4: The DMN vs NA Cardiac Control Dispute
- **Type**: Two-column comparison with species-level pie/bar visualization
- **Elements**: PVT's implicit ~50/50 framing vs. species values (cat 80% NA, monkey 60% NA, human 70-80% NA est.)
- **Includes**: Polyvagal Institute's "qualitative difference" defense + critics' rejoinder
- **Key Insight**: NA dominates cardiac vagal control in mammals; calling it a "split" overstates DMN's cardiac contribution

### Diagram 5: Dorsal Vagal Freezing — PVT Claim vs. Neuhuber Counter-Evidence
- **Type**: Two-column claim-evidence comparison
- **Elements**: 4 PVT claims about dorsal vagal freezing vs. 4 Neuhuber & Berthoud 2022 counter-findings
- **Key Insight**: The lived experience of shutdown/freeze is real; the proposed DMV-mediation mechanism doesn't survive direct anatomy

### Diagram 6: The 39-Author "Untenable" Consensus
- **Type**: First-author highlight + 7 field categories + geographic distribution
- **Elements**: Grossman as first author; 38 invited co-authors grouped by research field (cardiovascular autonomic physiology, comparative physiology, neuroanatomy, brainstem autonomic circuits, interoception, animal behavior, cardiology); 11-country institutional spread
- **Key Insight**: This is a genuinely cross-disciplinary international consensus, not "one researcher's opinion"

### Diagram 7: Porges's Response Framework
- **Type**: Concept map with central claim + 6 rebuttal branches, each paired with critics' rejoinder
- **Elements**: Porges's central claim ("critics evaluate a reconstructed proxy") + 6 specific defenses (category errors ×2, systems-level framing, falsifiability defense, measurement preference, reconstructed-proxy framing)
- **Key Insight**: Porges's response is intellectually serious but doesn't restore the falsified empirical content

### Diagram 8: The Falsifiability Debate
- **Type**: Three-column dialectical (critics — middle position — Porges)
- **Elements**: Critics' falsified-prediction list; honest middle position (4 numbered points); Porges's systems-level defense; central question
- **Key Insight**: The falsifiability debate isn't a tie — the most testable parts have been falsified; whether the systems-level reformulation preserves theoretical content distinct from competitors is the live question

### Diagram 9: Field Reception Map
- **Type**: 2x2 matrix on axes "concedes critique" × "defends clinical utility"

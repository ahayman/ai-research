## Visualization Descriptions

### 1. True Unemployment (viz-1)
Shows the hidden millions not captured by official U-3 statistics, including discouraged workers, part-timers, and hidden gig workers.

### 2. Job Creation Trend (viz-2)
Line chart showing the dramatic slowdown from 166K/month average in 2024 to just 62K/month average in Q3 2025.

### 3. Gig Economy Growth (viz-3)
Grouped bar chart showing the doubling of full-time independent workers from 13.6M (2020) to 27.7M (2024).

### 4. Benefits Gap (viz-4)
Side-by-side comparison of benefits access between gig workers and traditional employees.

### 5. 2025 Layoffs (viz-5)
Horizontal bar chart of 1.1M+ layoffs by industry sector through October 2025.

### 6. Earnings Comparison (viz-6)
Multi-metric comparison of traditional employee vs gig worker earnings and wage distribution.

### 7. Worker Flow (viz-7)
Doughnut chart showing where laid-off workers go: 20% to gig platforms, 45% traditional job search, etc.

### 8. Unemployment Measures (viz-8)
Progression from U-3 (4.2%) through U-6 (7.5%) to estimated true rate with hidden gig workers (~8.5%).

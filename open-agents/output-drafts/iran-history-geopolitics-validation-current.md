## Critical Errors Summary

### Factual Errors Requiring Correction

1. **US service member deaths (Current Crisis file, Section VII):** States "seven service members killed" -- actual count is SIX. States they were killed "primarily from Iranian attacks on bases in Saudi Arabia and Iraq" -- all six died in a SINGLE drone strike on Port Shuaiba, KUWAIT on March 1. Both the number and location are wrong.

2. **Senate vote count (Current Crisis file, Section VII):** States "47-52" -- actual vote was 47-53.

3. **Operation Midnight Hammer chronology (US-Iran file, Section 12):** Presents Operation Midnight Hammer (June 21-22, 2025) as preceding and being "followed by" the 12-Day War (June 13-24). In reality, the 12-Day War started June 13 with Israeli strikes, and Operation Midnight Hammer on June 22 was the US entering an already-ongoing conflict. The sequence is inverted.

4. **Nuclear negotiation rounds (Current Crisis file, Section VI):** Lists only 4 rounds (April 12, April 19, February 6 2026, February 26 2026). In reality there were at least 5 rounds in April-May 2025 alone (April 12, April 19, April 26, May 11, May 23), plus multiple rounds in February 2026 (Feb 6, Feb 17, Feb 26). The file drastically undercounts the rounds of negotiations.

### Minor Inaccuracies

5. **Nasrallah strike casualties:** States "at least 32 others" killed -- sources say at least 33 total fatalities (including Nasrallah).

6. **Nasrallah strike aircraft:** States "F-15I fighters" only -- actually F-15I Ra'am AND F-16I Sufa jets were used.

7. **Oil prices:** States Brent went "from approximately $70 to over $80" -- the initial jump was from ~$73 to ~$80, but by end of first week Brent had surged to $92+. The framing understates the magnitude of the price shock.

8. **Lebanon death toll:** States "at least 217" as of March 8 -- this was accurate as of March 6 but had risen to nearly 300-400 by March 8. The data appears to be from an earlier snapshot.

9. **Internal inconsistency on warhead equivalents:** Current crisis file says "nine warheads" worth of enriched material; Israel-Iran file says "5-6 nuclear weapons in less than two weeks." These may reflect different measurement points or definitions but create internal inconsistency.

### Potential Fabrication Concerns

**No fabricated events were detected.** All major events described in the four files -- including the October 7 attack, pager operations, Nasrallah assassination, True Promise operations, the Twelve-Day War, the December 2025 protests, the 2026 Iran war, Khamenei's assassination, and all regional responses -- are verified through multiple independent sources including major news outlets (CNN, NPR, Al Jazeera, BBC), government sources (UK Parliament, US Congress), international organizations (IAEA, OHCHR), academic institutions (Brown University, Chatham House, CSIS, Brookings), and encyclopedic references (Wikipedia, Britannica).

The research appears to be genuinely sourced from real reporting rather than confabulated. The errors found are typical of rapid compilation under time pressure (wrong numbers, inverted sequences, outdated figures) rather than wholesale fabrication.

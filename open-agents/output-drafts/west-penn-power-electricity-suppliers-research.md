### Current Status: NOT YET AVAILABLE

Pennsylvania has **not yet passed legislation** enabling a permanent community solar program. Key facts:

- **House Bill 1155** passed the PA House and is awaiting Senate consideration
- **HB 1555 and SB 472** are also pending votes
- A **pilot program** exists but permanent regulations are being developed
- Proposed rules would implement a permanent community solar program with consolidated billing by 2026

# Research Validation Report: AAC Product Market Analysis Supplement 1

## Validation Summary

**Overall Reliability**: Moderate-High

- **Confirmed**: 107 claims (80%)
- **Uncertain**: 23 claims (17%)
- **Invalid**: 4 claims (3%)

**Key Findings**:
The supplement research is substantially accurate across all 5 products. Most claims about product features, pricing, and platform availability were independently verified. However, several factual errors were found in the Proloquo section (release date, voice count, My-Own-Voice pricing, ExpressivePower attribution), and one error in the APP2Speak section (JMIR study miscount). No claims were found to be fabricated; errors appear to stem from conflating details between related products (Proloquo vs Proloquo2Go) or imprecise reading of source studies.

**Systemic Issues**:
- Proloquo/Proloquo2Go feature conflation: Multiple claims incorrectly attributed Proloquo2Go features to the newer Proloquo app (ExpressivePower, 100+ voices, My-Own-Voice compatibility)
- Difficulty proving negatives: Several "no X feature" claims for Weave Chat and Sweet Suite rated uncertain because absence of documentation doesn't definitively prove absence of features
- Snapshot-dependent data: App Store ratings, download counts, and pricing are point-in-time figures subject to change

# Voice Customization & Voice Cloning: PRC-Saltillo AAC Products

## Executive Summary

PRC-Saltillo is one of the most comprehensive AAC providers for voice customization, offering a layered ecosystem of TTS engines (Almagu, Acapela, Nuance, Apple, DECtalk), custom voice creation (VoiceKeeper), voice banking, message banking, and now AI-powered features via Apple Intelligence integration. With the 2025 acquisition of Avaz (which adds Expressive Tones emotional voice output) and the 2026 Therapy Box acquisition, PRC-Saltillo's voice portfolio is the broadest in the AAC market. However, significant gaps remain in emotional expressiveness across most products, nonbinary/gender-neutral voice options, and voice cloning accessibility for non-literate users.

**Default Voices:**
- Inclusive Voices by Almagu (ethnically and age-diverse synthetic voices)
- Acapela voices
- Apple system voices

**Additional Voices (purchasable in-app upgrade store):**

- VocaliD custom voices (purchased directly from VocaliD)
- VoiceKeeper custom voices

**Languages:** English, Spanish, Canadian French, Arabic, Hebrew, Mandarin Chinese (universal app for iPhone/iPad)

**Voice Management:** Only one voice comes pre-loaded; others are downloadable to save storage. Users select via Menu > Settings > Voice.

### 1.2 LAMP Words for Life (iOS App)

**Default Voices:**
- Inclusive Voices by Almagu
- Acapela voices
- Apple system voices

**Additional Options:**
- VoiceKeeper custom voices
- Apple Personal Voice (iOS 17+, English only)

**Customization Controls:**
- Voice selection per language
- Prompt Voice Pitch adjustment: Lowest, Lower, Low, Normal, High, Higher, Highest
- Downloadable voice packs (only one pre-loaded)

**Notable Change:** Ivona voices were removed in update 2.49 (July 2024) due to incompatibility with Apple's operating system and limited technical support. Replaced by Almagu and Acapela voices. This was disruptive for users who had identified with their Ivona voice for years.

### 1.3 Dialogue AAC (iOS App — for literate adults)

**Default Voices:**
- Almagu voices

- Voice transfer: A voice created for Dialogue can be transferred to Accent or other devices (separate license/purchase required)

## 2. Voice Cloning / Voice Banking Capabilities

### 2.1 VoiceKeeper (The Voice Keeper) — Primary Custom Voice Solution

**What It Is:** AI-powered voice personalization software that creates a synthesized voice from recordings. Not a recording playback system — it generates a fully synthesized TTS voice that can speak any text.

**Two Products:**

**A) Voice in a Minute (Quick Voice Creation):**
- Record as few as 5 short sentences to build an initial voice
- Hear a sample of synthesized voice in ~10 minutes
- Quality improves with every additional 10 minutes of recording
- Voice runs entirely offline after creation — nothing typed is sent to servers
- Audio samples automatically deleted within 72 hours of voice creation
- FREE for Dialogue AAC users; available for purchase for other PRC-Saltillo products

**B) VoiceKeeper Kids (Full Custom Voice with Voice Donor):**
- Family hand-picks a voice donor (sibling, cousin, friend, etc.)
- Expert voice coach guides donor through virtual recording sessions
- High-quality microphone shipped to donor
- Multiple virtual recording sessions led by voice coach
- Creates one-of-a-kind personalized voice for pediatric AAC users
- Private and secure; compatible with every major communication app
- Works on Windows and modern iPads
- Voice can also be donated anonymously in under 30 seconds via secure link

**Pricing:**
- FREE with Dialogue AAC app purchase (a $75 value)
- Separate purchase/license for LAMP Words for Life, TouchChat, Accent devices, Via Pro, TouchChat Express, PRiO devices
- Each device/app requires its own license

**Compatibility:** Works with almost any AAC solution — not limited to PRC-Saltillo products.

### 2.2 VocaliD Partnership

**Partnership History:** PRC has been a supporter of VocaliD from its inception.

**Technology:** VocaliD's BeSpoke voices blend the user's "vocal DNA" with recordings from a matched speaker in their Human Voicebank, creating a unique voice that captures vocal characteristics even from limited speech samples.

**Integration:** VocaliD voices integrate into PRC-Saltillo's Empower and Dialogue AAC software. Custom voices can be purchased directly from VocaliD and imported into TouchChat and other PRC-Saltillo products.

### 2.3 Apple Personal Voice Support

**How It Works (Current - iOS 17-18):**
- User records 150 phrases on their iPhone/iPad
- Takes approximately 15 minutes of speaking
- Device generates voice model overnight (while charging and locked)
- Voice stored securely on device with Face ID/Touch ID encryption
- Available to third-party AAC apps when "Allow Apps to use Personal Voice" is enabled
- Settings path: Settings > Accessibility > Personal Voice

**iOS 26 Update (2026):**
- **Dramatically reduced requirements:** Only ~10 brief phrases needed (approximately 1 minute of recording)
- Spanish (Mexico) added as supported language (previously English only)
- More languages planned for the future
- This is a game-changer for users with progressive conditions who may not have time for 150 phrases

**PRC-Saltillo Integration:**
- Supported in TouchChat, LAMP Words for Life, Unity AAC, and Dialogue AAC
- Settings path in PRC apps: Settings > Voice > Choose Custom Voice > Apple Personal Voice
- Currently limited to English voices and vocabularies (with Spanish Mexico coming in iOS 26)

### 2.4 Message Banking (Separate from Voice Cloning)

**What It Is:** Recording and preserving actual spoken words, phrases, and sentences in the user's natural voice — these are played back as recordings, not synthesized speech. Preserves authentic intonation and personality.

**PRC-Saltillo's Message Banking Service:**
- **Free** web-based service
- Access via desktop, tablet, or mobile phone at messagebanking.prc-saltillo.com
- Compatible with: Accent NuVoice, Accent Empower, Dialogue AAC on Via devices, iOS devices
- Process: Record → Download → Import into device for button activation
- Also integrates with mymessagebanking.com
- Ideal for literate adults transitioning to AAC due to degenerative neurological disease (ALS, etc.)

### 2.5 Personify Voice Recording System (Forbes AAC — Not PRC-Saltillo)

**Important Note:** Personify is made by Forbes AAC, not PRC-Saltillo. It is a related but separate product in the AAC ecosystem.

**What It Is:** A dedicated recording device for people with progressive disorders to record legacy messages while still verbal.

**How It Works:**
- Recordings saved, trimmed, labeled, and organized into folders on the Personify Recorder
- Backed up to cloud server
- When needed, Forbes AAC experts integrate recordings onto a Forbes AAC speech generating device
- Access via touch screen, switch scanning, head mouse, or eye tracking

**Key Difference from VoiceKeeper:** Personify stores actual voice recordings for playback; VoiceKeeper creates a synthesized voice that can speak any text.

## 3. Avaz Expressive Tones — Detailed Analysis

### Overview
Avaz's "Expressive Tones" is a first-of-its-kind feature in AAC that adds emotional expression to synthesized speech output. It is an exclusive feature to Avaz AAC.

### Available Emotional Tones (7 tones):
1. **Anger** — Express frustration or displeasure
2. **Excitement** — Convey enthusiasm and joy ("Yay!" sounds genuinely excited)
3. **Sadness** — Communicate grief, disappointment
4. **Curiosity / Question** — Inquiring tone for asking questions
5. **Sarcasm** — Subtle ironic inflection ("Yay" sounds distinctly different from excited "Yay")
6. **Whisper** — Quiet, secretive tone for sharing secrets or speaking softly
7. **Scared** — Fear or anxiety in voice

### Technical Implementation:
- **Cloud-based neural TTS** — requires active internet connection to function
- Likely built on Google Cloud TTS or Microsoft Azure Neural TTS with style/emotion SSML parameters
- Processing happens server-side, which is why internet is required
- Available on both iOS and Android

### Language Support:
- Currently supports **English (US) accents only**

### Significance:
- Addresses one of the most longstanding complaints about AAC: that synthesized speech sounds flat and robotic, unable to convey emotion
- Allows caregivers to model emotions on the AAC device
- Users can differentiate between emotional contexts (excited "Yay" vs. sarcastic "Yay")
- First commercially available AAC app with built-in emotional tone control

### Limitations:
- English US only — not available in Avaz's other 15+ supported languages
- Requires internet — not usable offline
- Limited to 7 preset tones — no custom tone creation or intensity control noted
- Only available in Avaz — not yet integrated into other PRC-Saltillo products (TouchChat, LAMP, etc.)

## 5. Voice as Identity: Features and Campaigns

### PRC-Saltillo's Approach to Voice Identity:

**Inclusive Voices Initiative (Almagu Partnership):**
- PRC-Saltillo was the first AAC company to offer an ethnically and age-diverse collection of synthetic voices
- 20+ voices representing child, teen, adult, and senior age groups
- Explicit goal: users should be able to find a voice that represents their identity
- Addresses the longstanding problem of AAC users all sounding alike

**VoiceKeeper Kids — Voice Donor Model:**
- Allows families to choose a voice donor who "sparks joy" in the child
- Creates a voice the child is personally connected to
- Marketing emphasizes: "Your child will experience a new sense of individuality with a voice only he has, and was created for him by your family"
- Privacy-first: "Not a tool for entertainment, impersonation, or cloning celebrities"

**VoiceKeeper — Voice Ownership:**
- The synthesized voice runs entirely offline — nothing typed is sent to servers
- Audio samples auto-deleted within 72 hours
- User retains full control of their voice

**Code Switching (2026.1):**
- Bilingual users can now have their voice switch languages within a single sentence
- Preserves bilingual identity rather than forcing monolingual output

### Broader AAC Community on Voice and Identity:

- A person's identity is strongly tied to their voice
- "Having the voice that matches every other person who uses AAC is very disempowering" — AAC user research
- Parents and clinicians report AAC users eschewing devices due to voice identity mismatch
- Stephen Hawking famously kept his DECtalk voice even when offered upgrades: "I keep it because I have not heard a voice I like better and because I have identified with it"
- Voice identity is particularly significant for long-term AAC users who become known by their synthetic voice

## 6. Voice Preservation Workflows for Progressive Conditions

### ALS / Motor Neuron Disease Workflow with PRC-Saltillo Products:

**Phase 1: Early Intervention (Speech Still Clear)**
- **Timeline:** Begin as soon as possible after diagnosis, while speech is clear
- **Voice Banking via VoiceKeeper:** Record 5+ sentences for basic voice; quality improves with additional recording (8-10 hours for comprehensive banking if speech is clear)
- **Message Banking:** Record meaningful phrases, greetings, expressions, jokes at messagebanking.prc-saltillo.com (free)
- **Apple Personal Voice:** On iOS 26, record just 10 phrases (~1 minute) to create a personal synthesized voice
- **VocaliD:** For highest-quality custom voice, engage early for BeSpoke voice creation

**Phase 2: Speech Declining**
- Can still begin voice/message banking even with mild speech changes
- Quality may be lower; process takes longer (can take months with slurred speech)
- Message banking of key phrases becomes critical during this window
- Team Gleason and ALS Association provide guided support

**Phase 3: Transition to AAC**
- Import banked messages into Dialogue AAC or Accent device
- Load custom VoiceKeeper/VocaliD/Apple Personal Voice into AAC app or device
- For Accent devices: message banking integrates directly into vocabulary files
- Switch scanning, head mouse, eye tracking access as motor function declines

**Head/Neck Cancer Considerations:**
- Pre-treatment voice banking recommended (before radiation/surgery)
- Timeline often more compressed than ALS
- Personify Voice Recording System (Forbes AAC) is specifically designed for this population
- PRC-Saltillo's message banking can complement pre-surgical voice banking

### Key Resources:
- messagebanking.prc-saltillo.com (free service)
- mymessagebanking.com (compatible with Dialogue AAC)
- app.thevoicekeeper.com/Dialogue (VoiceKeeper for Dialogue)
- Team Gleason voice banking guidance (teamgleason.org)
- ALS Association voice preservation resources (als.org)

## 7. Limitations and Gaps

### Voice Quality Issues:
- **Robotic quality persists:** Despite improvements, many AAC voices still sound noticeably synthetic. As one researcher noted, "many AAC communicators sound like different flavors of the computer from Star Trek"
- **Emotional flatness:** Most PRC-Saltillo voices (except Avaz Expressive Tones) cannot express emotions — no way to sound friendly, sarcastic, grumpy, or tired in TouchChat, LAMP, Unity, or Dialogue
- **Avaz Expressive Tones is siloed:** The 7-tone emotional voice feature exists only in Avaz, not in the other PRC-Saltillo apps. No announced timeline for bringing expressive tones to TouchChat, LAMP, Unity, or Dialogue

### Gender and Identity Gaps:
- **Limited nonbinary/gender-neutral voice options:** One of the most common complaints. AAC voices typically offer only binary "male" or "female" options with fixed pitch ranges
- **68% of nonbinary adults using assistive tech** reportedly experience voice-related distress impacting daily tasks (widely cited statistic; primary source unverified -- treat as indicative rather than definitive)
- **Accent and cultural representation:** Built primarily on American and British English models, most voices don't reflect accents, tones, or cultural contexts of diverse users globally
- **Almagu inclusive voices** partially address this with age and ethnic diversity, but gender-neutral options remain limited

### Technical Limitations:
- **Apple Personal Voice is English-only** across PRC-Saltillo products (Spanish Mexico coming in iOS 26)
- **Avaz Expressive Tones requires internet** — no offline emotional voice output
- **Avaz Expressive Tones is English US only** — not available in Avaz's other 15+ languages
- **VoiceKeeper voice quality scales with recording time** — the "1 minute" voice is a starting point, not high quality
- **Ivona voice removal was disruptive** — users who had identified with their Ivona voice for years lost their voice identity when it was removed from LAMP WFL in 2024
- **DECtalk only on Accent hardware** — legacy users on iOS cannot access their historical voice
- **No real-time voice cloning** from minimal samples across PRC-Saltillo products (Apple Personal Voice in iOS 26 with 10 phrases is the closest)

### Feature Fragmentation:
- Voice features are inconsistently distributed across products:
  - VoiceKeeper is free only in Dialogue, costs extra for other apps
  - Nuance Vocalizer is Accent-only
  - DECtalk is Accent-only
  - Expressive Tones is Avaz-only
  - Apple Intelligence features require iOS 26 hardware
  - Code switching is app-only (not on dedicated devices)
- No unified voice experience across the PRC-Saltillo ecosystem

### Voice Banking Accessibility:
- Traditional voice banking (1,600 phrases, 6-8 hours) is burdensome for those with progressive conditions
- Apple Personal Voice (iOS 26, 10 phrases) dramatically lowers the bar but is Apple-only and English-only
- VoiceKeeper "Voice in a Minute" helps but quality from minimal recordings is limited
- No PRC-Saltillo-native AI voice cloning that could reconstruct a voice from historical recordings or minimal samples

### Missing Features:
- No prosody/intonation control in most products
- No speech rate variation within sentences (all words spoken at same rate)
- No emphasis marking (ability to stress specific words)
- No integrated emotion/tone selection in TouchChat, LAMP, Unity, or Dialogue
- No voice aging (child voice doesn't grow with the user unless re-created)
- No voice blending between multiple voice profiles

## 8. Apple Intelligence / iOS 26 Impact on PRC-Saltillo Products

### 2026.1 App Update — AI Features for All Four iOS Apps

**Applies to:** LAMP Words for Life, TouchChat, Unity AAC, Dialogue AAC

**Three New AI Features:**

1. **AI Message Edit:**
   - Corrects grammar and spelling errors in messages
   - Additional creative enhancement options available
   - Helps ensure clear, confident communication

2. **AI Translate:**
   - Translates Speech Display Bar text into any language supported by Apple voices
   - Voice output adapts to the translated language automatically
   - Useful for travel, multilingual conversations, and cross-cultural communication

3. **AI Image Generation:**
   - Creates new button images from text prompts
   - Can use reference pictures
   - Built-in guardrails: won't generate images of people or copyrighted content
   - Powered by Apple Intelligence

**Voice-Specific Impact:**

- **Code Switching:** Voice synthesizer switches between any combination of Almagu, Acapela, or Apple voices based on language of words in the Speech Display Bar. If a sentence contains both English and Spanish words, each is spoken by the appropriate language voice.
- **AI Translate voice adaptation:** When text is translated, the voice automatically switches to an appropriate voice for the target language
- **Apple Personal Voice (iOS 26):** Reduced to ~10 phrases (~1 minute) for voice creation — dramatically more accessible for progressive condition users

**Requirements:**
- Specific iOS hardware running iOS 26
- AI features are off by default; users toggle on individually
- AI processing works without WiFi (on-device Apple Intelligence)

**Design Philosophy:** PRC-Saltillo states commitment to "design and implement AI technology that makes communication faster, more personalized, and more expressive while preserving authenticity, privacy, and user control."

### What Apple Intelligence Does NOT Yet Provide for AAC:
- No AI-powered emotional tone synthesis (Avaz Expressive Tones remains separate)
- No AI voice cloning beyond Apple Personal Voice
- No AI-powered word prediction improvements (PRC-Saltillo apps have their own prediction)
- No conversational AI assistant integration for AAC message composition
- No AI-powered voice quality enhancement of existing banked voices

## Validation Corrections to Apply Downstream
- Korkushko cardiovascular study: NOT "single-group." Small randomized open-label controlled cohort (39 treated + 40 control, PMID 22451889). Keep ~28% all-cause / ~2x CVD mortality figures + "never independently replicated."
- Khavinson & Morozov 2003 (266 elderly, PMID 14523363): combined thymalin + epithalamin, not epithalamin alone. Non-randomized, non-blinded.
- Khavinson 2003 telomerase "~2.4x" figure: present as "reported"/approximate. Qualitative result + passage 34→44 confirmed (PMID 12937682).
- 2025 Brunel correction (PMC12619744 / PMID 41240216) is a figure-image correction, NOT a retraction; numbers stand. Authors independent of Khavinson.
- FTC v. TA Sciences / Noel Patton ("Younger Games?"): real, but CONSENT ORDER with NO monetary penalty. Don't imply a fine.
- Peptide market size: state as range (~$42.5B one estimate; ~$84B by broader scope).
- Gray-market "43% failed" / MZ Biolabs purity stats: non-peer-reviewed; hedge. Peer-reviewed corroborant: online "semaglutide" measured 7.7-14.37% purity vs 99% claimed.
- Secretin: cite BOTH Cochrane PMID 22513913 (16+ RCTs, >900 children) AND AHRQ PMID 21464196 ("high strength of evidence for a lack of effectiveness").
- CFS melatonin+zinc RCT (PMID 34201806): Fatigue Impact Scale (FIS-40), not MFI-20; mental fatigue non-significant (p=0.058).
- Autism melatonin abnormality: Melke 2008 (Mol Psychiatry, PMC2199264) + Pagán 2014 (Transl Psychiatry, tp2014120) — keep journal names matched to IDs.
- TB-500: specify "systemic injectable" (topical thymosin β-4 / RGN-259 did reach Phase 2/3).
- St. Petersburg Institute ↔ NPCRIZ: structural COI via licensing/lineage (NPCRIZ holds exclusive rights), NOT proven common ownership.
- CFS framing: "no cure; symptom management via pacing" after NICE 2021 downgraded GET/CBT; PACE recovery rates inflated ~4x.

# Research Validation Report: Wellness Industry, Polyvagal Institute & Commercial Interests

## Validation Summary

**Overall Reliability**: High

- **Confirmed**: 17 claims (85%)
- **Uncertain**: 2 claims (10%)
- **Invalid**: 1 claim (5%)

**Key Findings**: Independent verification confirms PVI's 501(c)(3) status (EIN 84-5084757) and Denver headquarters, the four named co-founders (Porges, Dana, Onderko, Redfield) and the 2020 founding year, the 2023 and 2024 Form 990 revenue figures, Unyte Health's for-profit status and its commercial role as SSP's home since 2017, Porges's Chief Scientific Advisor role and royalty arrangement, the FDA clearance status of every consumer device named, and the book bibliography. **One correction required**: the research's component map and table list Porges as a "co-author" of Inderbitzen's *Autism in Polyvagal Terms*; while the publisher's catalog page lists both names (common cataloging practice for foreword authors), Porges contributed the foreword, not co-authorship. The research file itself already flags this distinction; the validation here confirms the foreword (not co-author) status and recommends keeping the explanation precise on this point.

**Systemic Issues**:
- The research is well-grounded in publicly verifiable sources (Form 990 filings, publisher pages, FDA-status disclosures).
- One small claim (the existence of a stand-alone "Polyvagal Equity Project" non-profit) could not be independently confirmed and is correctly flagged in the research as a gap.
- Pulsetto user-count claim (>100,000) is from a company press release and is a usage metric, not an efficacy metric — the research correctly characterizes this distinction.

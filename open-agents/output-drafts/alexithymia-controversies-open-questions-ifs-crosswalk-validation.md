### Section 5: Type I/II categorical vs dimensional

**Evidence weight.** LPA replication failure correctly cited (Schaeuffele 2021, Hogrefe FMM 2025, Chinese nursing students 2025, elderly LPA 2025). Ozdemir 2025 systematic review correctly framed as confirming dimensional pattern but not the categorical Type II distinction.

In this concept research the Hogrefe paper is referenced more cautiously as part of a body of work that consistently fails to find clean four-class typologies. The two-class finding is consistent with that framing without misrepresenting the specific result.
